Our Mission & Vision

Established for the expressed goal of carrying out activities that create and strengthen the unity of Eritrean and Ethiopian students at the University of Alberta and equally increase awareness about Eritrea and Ethiopia in the larger Edmonton community. We aim to further progress the community by events promoting education and unifying one another thus, leading to a friendly environment as well as increasing success within our community.
